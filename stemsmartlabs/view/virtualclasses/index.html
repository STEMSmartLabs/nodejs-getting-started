Virtual Classroom
Complete Solution

The biggest change that the education system globally has seen
this year is the massive shift to e-learning or digital classrooms.

Online learning

Educational institutions in most countries are now closed to contain the spread of coronavirus, resulting in the remote schooling of millions of students globally. Schools in India have been working hard in creating a comprehensive online infrastructure for the effective remote learning of their students.

Benefits of E-learning

The digital learning platforms enables the students with full access to the study material as well as allow them to engage in online classes and interact with the teachers like the physical classroom setting. The feedback is encouraging from both teachers and students. Adaptive learning is one of the techniques for providing personalized learning experiences, to enable effective and tailored learning paths to engage students and address their individual needs.

Sharing and managing resources

The platform allows the teacher to share resources in a variety of file formats with learners. A most useful feature is perhaps the ability to schedule the release of materials and assessment at specific times in the future.

Collaborative Learning

Virtual class room enables teachers to streamline classroom activities and implement a variety of teaching strategies consistent with modern pedagogic methods. Snapshots of exemplar worksheets and quiz are attached with the document

Assignment and Assessment

The only way to measure the effectiveness of a lesson is to measure students' learning outcome at the end of the class. Provision for online assignment and assessment in Classroom is perhaps the most useful feature as it allows the teacher to measure and track learners' progress and modify their teaching strategy as need be.




Managing grades

Much of the grade management is automatic but the teacher has full control over changing grades as need be. Classroom gives the ability to export grades from assignments to Sheets.

Live Recording

Live Classes can be recorded and stored in the cloud server, this can be playback later by students anytime. The classroom allows the teacher to incorporate video into assignment and quiz and design questions around it. The video can be played as the learner is working on the assignment or the quiz.

Managing people in class

The app gives the teacher complete authority to remove anyone from the class. This is an important provision as it allows the teacher to remove suspicious accounts that may compromise the online safety and privacy of learners.
